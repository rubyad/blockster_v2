About this event

An evening with the Moonpay product team

Once a quarter the Moonpay team takes over the Soho House rooftop in Meatpacking, opens the bar, and lets the Solana NYC community ask anything. This time we're focused on the new card flow and what it means for stablecoin payouts in emerging markets.

Two short talks (15 minutes each, max). The rest of the night is drinks, demos, and the kind of conversations you only have when everyone in the room actually ships things.

Casual dress. Bring a friend (just RSVP a +1). The first 50 people through the door get a Moonpay × Blockster pin.

About this event

A night of generative sound and physical bass

Memetic AI Records is a small Berlin label putting out music made by humans collaborating with models. Once a year they take over Klein Hamburg, a 600-capacity warehouse in Kreuzberg, and run a single night of eight live AI × synth acts. This year is the fourth.

Soundsystem is a custom Funktion-One rig built for the room. Lights are by the Mixed Reality crew. The bar runs all night. The dance floor doesn't sit down.

Phones go in pouches at the door. You'll get them back at the end. Photo and video by the resident crew only — they'll post it the next day.
