Clear space

Give it room

Maintain at least the height of the lime circle as clear space on every side of the wordmark. Don't crowd it with other elements.

Don't

Things we don't do

  • Don't change the font · always Inter 800 uppercase
  • Don't recolor the lime circle to anything other than #CAFC00
  • Don't add a drop shadow, gradient, or stroke
  • Don't rotate, skew, or stretch the wordmark
  • Don't place the lime icon on a busy photo without a backing layer
  • Don't pair the wordmark with another logo without space between

One-line tagline

Do
  • Write with the same precision you'd want from a Bloomberg terminal
  • Use mono font for all numbers, addresses, and dates
  • Lead with the data, then the human
  • Sentence case in headlines
  • Treat lime #CAFC00 as an accent — not a button background on light pages
Don't
  • Don't use emojis in production text — ever
  • Don't say "to the moon", "wagmi", "gm fren", or any of that
  • Don't promise gains. Don't shill. Don't financialize the vibe
  • Don't use random greens for "earning" — only lime #CAFC00
  • Don't use Title Case in headlines
